# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students at the College of Computing who need to check their personal exam schedule quickly from a shared Google Sheet.

## Product Purpose

Provide a fast, low-friction way to look up an individual exam schedule by student ID and sheet link. Success looks like effortless lookup with minimal steps and no confusion.

## Brand Personality

Clear, easy, efficient.

## Anti-references

Messy or cluttered interfaces, unnecessary decoration, or dense layouts that slow down lookup.

## Design Principles

- Reduce steps to the shortest path from input to result.
- Make status and outcomes obvious at a glance.
- Keep the interface calm and uncluttered so the task feels effortless.
- Prioritize readability over decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Support reduced motion, maintain strong color contrast, and avoid color-only status cues.
